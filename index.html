Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

Borgo is built on trust. We collect only what's needed to coordinate rides for your child, and we never sell your data.

1. What We Collect

When you use Borgo, we may collect:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Profile information — your phone number and home address, used to coordinate pickups.
  • Roster information — player names and parent contact details imported by a coach from their team roster.
  • Ride data — events, ride requests, confirmations, and responses to ride check messages.
  • SMS replies — when a parent replies YES or NO to a ride check, we record that response to update the coach dashboard.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send ride check SMS messages to parents on behalf of their team coach.
  • Update coaches in real time when a parent responds.
  • Notify co-parents and secondary contacts about ride confirmations.
  • Send reminders before confirmed rides.
  • Improve the Borgo app and fix issues.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell your data to any third party.

3. SMS Messaging

Borgo sends SMS messages to phone numbers provided by team coaches as part of their roster. These messages are used solely to coordinate transportation for youth sports events. Parents may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Standard message and data rates may apply.

4. Who We Share Data With

We share data only with the services necessary to operate Borgo:

  • Twilio — for sending and receiving SMS messages. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy.
  • Supabase — for database and authentication services.

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the address below. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request.

6. Children's Privacy

Borgo is designed for use by parents and coaches of youth athletes. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Player information (names and ages) is provided by coaches and parents solely for the purpose of coordinating transportation.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure authentication, and restricted access to personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of Borgo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
