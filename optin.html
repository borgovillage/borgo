🏘️ What is Borgo?

📋 Ride checks — Before events, your coach sends a quick text asking if your child needs a ride. Reply YES or NO — no app needed.
🚗 Ride coordination — When rides are confirmed, both parents get notified automatically. No more wondering if your kid has a ride.
🔒 Your team only — Messages come from your coach, for your team's events. Never used for marketing or shared with third parties.

📱 What messages will I receive?

Example — Ride Check
Hi Sarah, this is Coach Mike from Borgo, your team's carpool app. Does Emma need a ride to Lightning FC Practice on Sat Mar 21 at 11:00 AM? Reply YES if she needs a ride, or NO if she's sorted. Reply STOP to opt out. Msg & data rates may apply.
Example — Ride Confirmed
Borgo: A ride has been confirmed for Emma to Lightning FC Practice on Sat Mar 21. The Johnson family will pick her up. Reply STOP to opt out. Msg & data rates may apply.

Want to opt out before receiving any texts?

You don't need to submit this form to opt out. Simply contact your coach and ask to be removed from the Borgo roster. If you've already received a text, reply STOP to any message at any time. You will not receive further messages.
